## Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

### Survival by Gender

The analysis showed that female passengers had a significantly higher survival count than male passengers.

### Passenger Class Distribution

Most passengers belonged to Third Class (Pclass 3), indicating that the majority of travelers were from the lower passenger class category.

### Age Distribution

Most passengers were between 20 and 40 years old, making this the most common age group among Titanic travelers.

## Conclusion

The Titanic dataset revealed important patterns related to passenger survival. Gender and passenger class had a strong influence on survival rates, while the age distribution showed that most travelers were young adults.
